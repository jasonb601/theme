Pope Fiction! The Best Band Ever!

On Thursday, April 9th, 2015, the band, Pope Fiction, has been named the greatest band in the world. The band made their debut performance at Severna Park High School's Battle of the Bands. Unfortunately they did not win, but they were a close sixth. Just before their school's spring break, they released their debut album with Hopeless Record, Quiet Skunk. The album features hit songs like Empty Houses, Bulletproof Slime, Miss Saggy, and Emo Clown Jungles. Following the release of their album, they began a week tour over spring break. The band tried to play at Severna Park High Schoool's S.T.A.R. Week, but failed to get in due to administration issues. The band is was desperately trying to find shows after loosing their manager, Michelle Hickman. On Wednesday, April 15th, 2015, the band recruited Quinton's father as their new manager. He immediatly was on the phone with many contacts and managed to get the band a spot at Coachella 2016, opening up for their former member who is now at large, Danny Millan. The band also is said to appear in scenes of upcoming films such as Something Worth Remembering 2: The Sister Sequel, Heist pt II, Frogman, The Movie, and Fast and Furious 14. Additionally, the band will appear on The Ellen DeGeneres Show,The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, Late Show With David Letterman, Dr. Phil, and Oprah.
